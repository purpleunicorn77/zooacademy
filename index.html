✦ Virtual Mind Shelter ✦

STARGAZING offers instant communication support and a contextual atmosphere.

Professions: User Interface, color theory, and product design.

Tools: Illustrator, Visual Studio Code.

Research


✦ Online fortune-telling platforms driven by current trends are gaining popularity.

✦ Astrology and metaphysics have become increasingly fashionable in recent times.

✦ Stress levels in people's daily lives are on the rise.

✦ Mobile apps can offer instant support and assistance to individuals struggling with mental health challenges.


Reference


✦ Apps such as Hint for Zodiac Signs: Serene, 測測, Nebula: Horoscope & Astrology, and others.


Conceptualize


✦ Offering easy access and insightful guidance to help alleviate life’s stresses.

✦ Delivering the most practical and relevant features across six key functions.

✦ Maintaining a cohesive color palette and consistent graphic design.

✦ Enhancing user interaction through connections with real people and AI assistance within the app.


Colour Palette and Font


✦ After exploring various colour options, I found that soft, uplifting, and enigmatic tones are the most appealing choices, harmonizing beautifully with the background theme.


✦ Kefa exudes elegance and modernity. Its design perfectly aligns with STARGAZING, embodying both mystery and depth.


Design System

✦ I designed a system featuring a series of buttons, a popup for explanations, a general article reading section, a trophy-based unlocking mechanism, a conversation area, and an integrated payment method.

Today's Luck

Today's Luck provides predictive insights on Love, Fortune, and Career, offering guidance on what to be mindful of and suggestions for potential actions to take.

Prediction

Prediction feature offers AI-generated answers to user queries through 'Dice' or 'Card' tools. If further clarity is needed, users can access the 'ASK' service, where real professionals provide detailed responses. This service incurs a fee based on the professionals' rates.

Astrology

Astrology allows users to explore their star signs, offering deeper understanding into their personalities and helping them better understand others. It serves as a fascinating tool for self-discovery.

Tarot

A spread of the 22 Major Arcana cards invites participants to choose 4 cards, representing 'Now,' 'Future,' 'Yourself,' and 'Someone.' The interpretation provides guidance and insights into the event or situation causing concern.

Life Map

Life Map personalizes an individual's life journey, offering details into their star sign, including the positioning of stars in specific houses and their interactions. This clear and straightforward approach can serve as a valuable reference, such as identifying areas of career interest aligned with the user's strengths and preferences.

Mood Test

Mood Test is a psychological assessment inspired by research suggesting that past memories shape people’s predictions about the future, influence their current experiences, and guide decisions with significant short- and long-term effects. By answering 5 questions, participants gain insights into their thoughts and feelings.
